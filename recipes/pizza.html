Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100ml passata
  • fresh basil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 125g mozzarella
  • handful of parmesan
  • handful of cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt.
  2. Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough.
  3. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 mins until smooth.
  4. Cover with a tea towel and set aside. You can leave the dough to rise if you like, but it is not essential for a thin crust.
  5. Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste.
  6. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.
  7. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin.
  8. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven.
  9. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  10. heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8.
  11. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon.
  12. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season.
  13. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray.
  14. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp.
  15. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil leaves if using.
