Banana Bread

Banana bread is a type of cake made from mashed banana

Ingredients

  • 140g butter, softened, plus extra for the tin
  • 140g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 140g self raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 50g icing sugar
  • handful dry banana chips for decoration

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 160 degrees C, fan.
  2. Butter a loaf tin and line base and sides with baking parchment.
  3. Cream 140g butter and 140g caster sugar.
  4. Add 2 beaten large eggs with a little of the self-raising flour.
  5. Fold the reamining flour, baking powder and mashed bananas.
  6. Pour the mixture in the tin and bake for 50mn.
  7. Cool in the tin for 10 mn thenplace on a wire rack.
  8. Mix icing sugar with 2-3 tsp of water for a runny icing.
  9. Drizzle the icing on top of the cake and decorate with a handful of banana chips.
