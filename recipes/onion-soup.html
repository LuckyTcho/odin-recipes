Onion Soup

Onion soup is a soup usually based on meat stock and onions, and often served gratin√©ed with croutons or a larger piece of bread covered with cheese floating on top

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 kg onions sliced
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 250ml dry white wine
  • 1.3l hot beef stock
  • 4-8 slices baguette
  • 140g grated gruyere

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with olive oil in a large pan.
  2. Add the onions and fry with the lid on for 10 mn until soft.
  3. Sprinkle the sugar and cokk for 20 mn more, stirring frequently unitl caramelised.
  4. Add the garlic cloves for the last few minutes of the onions cooking time.
  5. Sprinkle the plain flour and stir well.
  6. Increase the heat and keep stirring as you gradually add in the wine, followed by the beef stock.
  7. Cover and simmer for 15-20 mn.
  8. To serve, turn on the grill and toast the bread.
  9. Put a slice or two of toast on top of the bowls of soup.
  10. Pile on the gruyere, grill until melted.
